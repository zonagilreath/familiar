# Cleric

**Hit Die:** d8 | **Saves:** Wisdom, Charisma | **Armor/Weapons:** Light & Medium armor, Shields, Simple weapons

**Divine Order (Level 1 choice):** Protector -- adds Martial weapon proficiency and Heavy armor training. Thaumaturge -- extra cantrip and WIS mod bonus to Arcana/Religion checks.

## Core Mechanics

**Channel Divinity** (uses: 2 at level 2, 3 at level 6, 4 at level 18; recharge 1/Short Rest, all/Long Rest):
- **Divine Spark** (Magic action, 30 ft.): Roll 1d8 + WIS mod. Heal an ally that amount OR force a CON save for Necrotic/Radiant damage (half on success). Scales: 2d8 at level 7, 3d8 at level 13, 4d8 at level 18.
- **Turn Undead** (Magic action, 30 ft.): Each Undead makes WIS save or is Frightened + Incapacitated for 1 minute. Ends on damage.

**Spellcasting:** Full caster (WIS). Prepares from full Cleric list daily. Standard full-caster spell slot progression.

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Spellcasting, Divine Order (Protector or Thaumaturge)
- **Level 2:** Channel Divinity (Divine Spark, Turn Undead)
- **Level 3:** Cleric Subclass
- **Level 5:** Sear Undead -- Turn Undead also deals WIS mod d8s of Radiant damage to failed Undead
- **Level 7:** Blessed Strikes (choose one):
  - Divine Strike: +1d8 Necrotic/Radiant on weapon hit, 1/turn
  - Potent Spellcasting: add WIS mod to cantrip damage
- **Level 10:** Divine Intervention (Magic action): Cast any Cleric spell of level 5 or lower without a slot or material components. 1/Long Rest
- **Level 14:** Improved Blessed Strikes -- Divine Strike increases to 2d8 OR Potent Spellcasting grants 2x WIS mod Temp HP when dealing cantrip damage
- **Level 20:** Greater Divine Intervention -- can choose Wish with Divine Intervention (can't reuse for 2d4 Long Rests)

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Life Domain
Premier healer. Disciple of Life (Level 3) adds 2 + spell slot level HP to any spell-slot healing. Preserve Life (Level 3 Channel Divinity, Magic action) distributes 5x Cleric level HP among Bloodied creatures within 30 ft. (max half their HP). Blessed Healer (Level 6) heals the Cleric for 2 + slot level when healing others. Supreme Healing (Level 17) maximizes all healing dice from spells and Channel Divinity. Always-prepared spells include Aid, Bless, Cure Wounds, Revivify, Death Ward, Mass Cure Wounds.
