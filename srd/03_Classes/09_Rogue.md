# Rogue

**Hit Die:** d8 | **Saves:** Dexterity, Intelligence | **Armor/Weapons:** Light armor, Simple weapons, Martial Finesse/Light weapons, Thieves' Tools

## Core Mechanics

**Sneak Attack** (1/turn): Extra damage when attacking with Finesse or Ranged weapon AND you have Advantage, or an un-Incapacitated ally is within 5 ft. of target (and you don't have Disadvantage). Same damage type as the weapon.

|-------|-------------------|
| 1-2   | 1d6               |
| 3-4   | 2d6               |
| 5-6   | 3d6               |

**Extra ASIs:** Rogues get ASIs at levels 4, 8, 10, 12, and 16.

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Expertise (2 skills), Sneak Attack (1d6), Weapon Mastery (2 weapons)
- **Level 2:** Cunning Action (Bonus Action: Dash, Disengage, or Hide)
- **Level 3:** Rogue Subclass, Steady Aim (Bonus Action: gain Advantage on next attack this turn; Speed becomes 0, can't have moved this turn)
- **Level 5:** Cunning Strike (on Sneak Attack, forgo dice for effects, save DC = 8 + DEX mod + Prof):
  - Poison (1d6 cost): CON save or Poisoned for 1 min (requires Poisoner's Kit)
  - Trip (1d6 cost): DEX save or Prone (Large or smaller)
  - Withdraw (1d6 cost): move half Speed without provoking
- **Level 5:** Uncanny Dodge (Reaction: halve attack damage from visible attacker)
- **Level 7:** Evasion (DEX saves: no damage on success, half on fail), Reliable Talent (minimum 10 on proficient ability checks)
- **Level 11:** Improved Cunning Strike -- can apply two Cunning Strike effects per Sneak Attack
- **Level 14:** Devious Strikes (new Cunning Strike options):
  - Daze (2d6 cost): CON save or can only move, act, or Bonus Action on next turn
  - Knock Out (6d6 cost): CON save or Unconscious for 1 min (save each turn, ends on damage)
  - Obscure (3d6 cost): DEX save or Blinded until end of next turn
- **Level 15:** Slippery Mind -- proficiency in WIS and CHA saves
- **Level 18:** Elusive -- no attack roll has Advantage against you (unless Incapacitated)
- **Level 20:** Stroke of Luck -- turn any failed d20 Test into a 20. 1/Short or Long Rest

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Thief
Classic dungeon delver. Fast Hands (Level 3) allows Sleight of Hand, Use Object, or use a magic item as a Bonus Action. Second-Story Work (Level 3) grants Climb Speed = Speed and DEX-based jump distance. Supreme Sneak (Level 9) adds a Cunning Strike option: Stealth Attack (1d6 cost) to maintain Invisible condition after attacking if ending turn behind 3/4 or Total Cover. Use Magic Device (Level 13) allows attunement to 4 items, chance to not expend magic item charges (roll 6 on d6), and use of any Spell Scroll (INT-based). Thief's Reflexes (Level 17) grants two turns in the first round of combat (normal Initiative and Initiative - 10).
