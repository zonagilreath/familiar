# Barbarian

**Hit Die:** d12 | **Saves:** Strength, Constitution | **Armor/Weapons:** Light & Medium armor, Shields, Simple & Martial weapons

## Core Mechanics

**Rage** (Bonus Action, can't wear Heavy armor): Resistance to Bludgeoning/Piercing/Slashing damage, bonus damage on Strength attacks (see scaling below), Advantage on STR checks/saves. Can't concentrate or cast spells. Lasts up to 10 minutes; must extend each round by attacking, forcing a save, or using a Bonus Action.

| Level | Rages | Rage Damage | Weapon Masteries |
|-------|-------|-------------|------------------|
| 1-2   | 2     | +2          | 2                |
| 3-4   | 3     | +2          | 2-3              |
| 5-8   | 3-4   | +2          | 3                |
| 9-12  | 4-5   | +3          | 3-4              |
| 13-15 | 5     | +3          | 4                |
| 16-18 | 5-6   | +4          | 4                |
| 19-20 | 6     | +4          | 4                |

**Unarmored Defense:** AC = 10 + DEX mod + CON mod (can use Shield).

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Rage, Unarmored Defense, Weapon Mastery (2 weapons)
- **Level 2:** Danger Sense (Advantage on DEX saves), Reckless Attack (Advantage on STR attacks for a turn, but attacks against you also have Advantage)
- **Level 3:** Barbarian Subclass
- **Level 5:** Extra Attack, Fast Movement (+10 ft. Speed, no Heavy armor)
- **Level 7:** Feral Instinct (Advantage on Initiative), Instinctive Pounce (move half Speed when entering Rage)
- **Level 9:** Brutal Strike -- forgo Reckless Attack Advantage on one attack to deal +1d10 damage and apply Forceful Blow (push 15 ft. + chase) or Hamstring Blow (reduce Speed by 15 ft.)
- **Level 11:** Relentless Rage -- when dropped to 0 HP while Raging, DC 10 CON save to drop to (2 x Barbarian level) HP instead. DC increases by 5 each use; resets on Short/Long Rest
- **Level 13:** Improved Brutal Strike -- adds Staggering Blow (Disadvantage on next save, no Opportunity Attacks) and Sundering Blow (+5 to next ally's attack roll)
- **Level 15:** Persistent Rage -- regain all Rage uses on Initiative roll (1/Long Rest); Rage auto-extends for 10 min
- **Level 17:** Improved Brutal Strike -- damage increases to 2d10, can apply two effects per Brutal Strike
- **Level 18:** Indomitable Might -- STR checks/saves can't roll below your Strength score
- **Level 20:** Primal Champion -- STR and CON increase by 4 (max 25)

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Path of the Berserker
Fury-focused melee combatant. Frenzy (Level 3) adds extra damage dice (equal to Rage Damage bonus in d6s) on the first Strength attack each turn while using Reckless Attack during Rage. Mindless Rage (Level 6) grants Immunity to Charmed/Frightened while Raging. Retaliation (Level 10) allows a melee attack as a Reaction when taking damage from a creature within 5 ft. Intimidating Presence (Level 14) is a Bonus Action 30-ft. AoE Frighten (WIS save, DC = 8 + STR mod + Prof).
