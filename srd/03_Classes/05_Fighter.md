# Fighter

**Hit Die:** d10 | **Saves:** Strength, Constitution | **Armor/Weapons:** All armor, Shields, Simple & Martial weapons

## Core Mechanics

**Second Wind** (Bonus Action): Regain 1d10 + Fighter level HP. Uses scale: 2 (level 1), 3 (level 4), 4 (level 10). Recharge 1/Short Rest, all/Long Rest.

**Action Surge** (1/Short Rest): Take one additional action on your turn (not Magic action). Two uses at level 17 (still only once per turn).

**Weapon Mastery:** 3 weapons at level 1, scaling to 6 at level 16.

**Extra ASIs:** Fighters get ASIs at levels 4, 6, 8, 12, 14, and 16 (more than other classes).

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Fighting Style (feat), Second Wind, Weapon Mastery (3 weapons)
- **Level 2:** Action Surge (1 use), Tactical Mind (expend Second Wind use to add 1d10 to failed ability check; not expended if still fails)
- **Level 3:** Fighter Subclass
- **Level 5:** Extra Attack (2 attacks), Tactical Shift (move half Speed without provoking when using Second Wind)
- **Level 9:** Indomitable (1/Long Rest) -- reroll failed save with bonus = Fighter level; 2 uses at level 13, 3 at level 17. Tactical Master -- replace weapon mastery property with Push, Sap, or Slow
- **Level 11:** Two Extra Attacks (3 attacks per Attack action)
- **Level 13:** Studied Attacks -- if you miss an attack, gain Advantage on your next attack against that creature before end of next turn
- **Level 17:** Action Surge (2 uses), Indomitable (3 uses)
- **Level 20:** Three Extra Attacks (4 attacks per Attack action)

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Champion
Critical hit specialist. Improved Critical (Level 3) crits on 19-20. Remarkable Athlete (Level 3) gives Advantage on Initiative and Athletics; move half Speed on crit with no Opportunity Attacks. Additional Fighting Style (Level 7). Heroic Warrior (Level 10) grants Heroic Inspiration at the start of each combat turn if you lack it. Superior Critical (Level 15) crits on 18-20. Survivor (Level 18) grants Advantage on Death Saves (18-20 counts as 20) and regenerates 5 + CON mod HP at start of each turn while Bloodied with 1+ HP.
