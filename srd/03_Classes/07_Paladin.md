# Paladin

**Hit Die:** d10 | **Saves:** Wisdom, Charisma | **Armor/Weapons:** All armor, Shields, Simple & Martial weapons

## Core Mechanics

**Lay On Hands** (Bonus Action): Pool of healing = 5x Paladin level HP. Touch to heal any amount from pool. Spend 5 HP from pool to remove Poisoned condition.

**Spellcasting:** Half caster (CHA), spell slots up to 5th level. Standard half-caster progression.

**Channel Divinity** (uses: 2 at level 3, 3 at level 11; recharge 1/Short Rest, all/Long Rest):
- **Divine Sense** (Bonus Action): Detect Celestials, Fiends, and Undead within 60 ft. for 10 min.
- Subclass options (see below)

**Paladin's Smite** (Level 2): Always have Divine Smite prepared. Can cast it once without a spell slot (1/Long Rest).

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Lay On Hands, Spellcasting, Weapon Mastery (2 weapons)
- **Level 2:** Fighting Style, Paladin's Smite (free Divine Smite 1/Long Rest)
- **Level 3:** Channel Divinity, Paladin Subclass
- **Level 5:** Extra Attack, Faithful Steed (always have Find Steed prepared; 1 free cast/Long Rest)
- **Level 6:** Aura of Protection (10-ft. Emanation) -- you and allies add CHA mod (min +1) to all saving throws
- **Level 9:** Abjure Foes (Channel Divinity, Magic action) -- CHA mod creatures within 60 ft. make WIS save or Frightened for 1 min (can only move, act, or Bonus Action -- not all three)
- **Level 10:** Aura of Courage -- you and allies in Aura of Protection are Immune to Frightened
- **Level 11:** Radiant Strikes -- +1d8 Radiant on every melee weapon/Unarmed Strike hit
- **Level 14:** Restoring Touch -- Lay On Hands can also remove Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned (5 HP from pool per condition)
- **Level 18:** Aura Expansion -- Aura of Protection extends to 30 ft.

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Oath of Devotion
Guardian against evil. Always-prepared spells include Protection from Evil and Good, Shield of Faith, Aid, Beacon of Hope, Freedom of Movement, Guardian of Faith, Flame Strike. Sacred Weapon (Level 3 Channel Divinity) imbues a melee weapon with +CHA mod to attack rolls and Radiant damage option for 10 min. Aura of Devotion (Level 7) grants Charm Immunity in Aura of Protection. Smite of Protection (Level 15) grants Half Cover in the aura when casting Divine Smite. Holy Nimbus (Level 20, Bonus Action, 10 min) deals CHA mod + Prof Radiant damage to enemies starting their turn in the aura, Advantage on saves vs. Fiends/Undead, and fills the aura with sunlight.
