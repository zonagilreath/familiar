# Ranger

**Hit Die:** d10 | **Saves:** Strength, Dexterity | **Armor/Weapons:** Light & Medium armor, Shields, Simple & Martial weapons

## Core Mechanics

**Favored Enemy:** Always have Hunter's Mark prepared. Free casts (no spell slot): 2 at level 1, scaling to 6 at level 17. Recharge all free casts on Long Rest.

**Spellcasting:** Half caster (WIS), spell slots up to 5th level. Standard half-caster progression.

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Spellcasting, Favored Enemy (Hunter's Mark, 2 free casts), Weapon Mastery (2 weapons)
- **Level 2:** Deft Explorer (Expertise in 1 skill), Fighting Style
- **Level 3:** Ranger Subclass
- **Level 5:** Extra Attack
- **Level 6:** Roving (+10 ft. Speed without Heavy armor, gain Climb and Swim Speed equal to Speed)
- **Level 10:** Tireless (Magic action: 1d8 + WIS mod Temp HP, WIS mod uses/Long Rest)
- **Level 13:** Relentless Hunter -- damage can't break Concentration on Hunter's Mark
- **Level 14:** Nature's Veil (Bonus Action): become Invisible until end of next turn. WIS mod uses/Long Rest
- **Level 17:** Precise Hunter -- Advantage on attacks against creature marked by Hunter's Mark
- **Level 18:** Feral Senses -- Blindsight 30 ft.
- **Level 20:** Foe Slayer -- Hunter's Mark damage die increases to d10

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Hunter
Versatile monster slayer. Hunter's Lore (Level 3) reveals Immunities, Resistances, and Vulnerabilities of Hunter's Mark target. Hunter's Prey (Level 3, choose one, swappable on rest): Colossus Slayer (+1d8 damage to wounded targets, 1/turn) or Horde Breaker (free extra attack against a different creature within 5 ft. of original target). Defensive Tactics (Level 7, choose one, swappable): Escape the Horde (Opportunity Attacks have Disadvantage) or Multiattack Defense (after being hit, that creature has Disadvantage on further attacks this turn). Superior Hunter's Prey (Level 11) applies Hunter's Mark extra damage to a second creature within 30 ft. of the first, 1/turn. Superior Hunter's Defense (Level 15, Reaction): gain Resistance to the triggering damage type until end of turn.
