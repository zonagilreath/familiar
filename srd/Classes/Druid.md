# Druid

**Hit Die:** d8 | **Saves:** Intelligence, Wisdom | **Armor/Weapons:** Light armor, Shields, Simple weapons

**Primal Order (Level 1 choice):** Magician -- extra cantrip, WIS mod bonus to Arcana/Nature checks. Warden -- Martial weapon proficiency and Medium armor training.

## Core Mechanics

**Wild Shape** (Bonus Action; uses: 2 at level 2, 3 at level 6, 4 at level 17; recharge 1/Short Rest, all/Long Rest): Transform into a known Beast form for hours = half Druid level. Gain Temp HP = Druid level. Retain INT/WIS/CHA, class features, HP, and Hit Dice. Can't cast spells in Beast form (until level 18). Can also expend a use to cast Find Familiar (Wild Companion).

| Druid Level | Known Forms | Max CR | Fly Speed |
|-------------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| 2           | 4           | 1/4    | No        |
| 4           | 6           | 1/2    | No        |
| 8           | 8           | 1      | Yes       |

**Spellcasting:** Full caster (WIS). Prepares from full Druid list daily. Standard full-caster spell slot progression.

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Spellcasting, Druidic (always have Speak with Animals prepared), Primal Order
- **Level 2:** Wild Shape, Wild Companion (expend Wild Shape or spell slot to cast Find Familiar)
- **Level 3:** Druid Subclass
- **Level 5:** Wild Resurgence -- expend a spell slot to regain a Wild Shape use (1/turn); or expend a Wild Shape use for a level 1 slot (1/Long Rest)
- **Level 7:** Elemental Fury (choose one):
  - Potent Spellcasting: add WIS mod to cantrip damage
  - Primal Strike: +1d8 Cold/Fire/Lightning/Thunder on weapon or Beast form attack, 1/turn
- **Level 15:** Improved Elemental Fury -- Potent Spellcasting adds 300 ft. range to 10+ ft. cantrips OR Primal Strike increases to 2d8
- **Level 18:** Beast Spells -- can cast spells in Wild Shape (except spells with costly/consumed Material components)
- **Level 20:** Archdruid -- regain 1 Wild Shape on Initiative if at 0 uses; convert Wild Shape uses to spell slots (2 levels per use, 1/Long Rest)

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Circle of the Land
Terrain-themed caster with expanded spell access. Choose a land type (Arid/Polar/Temperate/Tropical) on each Long Rest for bonus prepared spells. Land's Aid (Level 3, Magic action, expend Wild Shape) creates a 10-ft. sphere at 60 ft. dealing 2d6 Necrotic (CON save, half on success) and healing one creature 2d6; scales to 3d6 at level 10, 4d6 at level 14. Natural Recovery (Level 6) recovers spell slots on Short Rest (up to half Druid level in combined slot levels, no level 6+). Nature's Ward (Level 10) grants Poison immunity and damage Resistance matching land type (Arid=Fire, Polar=Cold, Temperate=Lightning, Tropical=Poison). Nature's Sanctuary (Level 14, Magic action, expend Wild Shape) creates a movable 15-ft. Cube granting Half Cover and the land's damage Resistance to allies.
