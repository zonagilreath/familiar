# Bard

**Hit Die:** d8 | **Saves:** Dexterity, Charisma | **Armor/Weapons:** Light armor, Simple weapons

## Core Mechanics

**Bardic Inspiration** (Bonus Action, 60 ft.): Give an ally a die they can add to a failed d20 Test within 1 hour. Uses = CHA mod (min 1), recharge on Long Rest (Short Rest at level 5). One die per creature at a time.

| Level | Bardic Die | Spell Slots (highest) |
|-------|------------|-----------------------|
| 1-4   | d6         | 1st-2nd               |
| 5-9   | d8         | 2nd-5th               |
| 10-14 | d10        | 5th-7th               |
| 15-20 | d12        | 8th-9th               |

**Spellcasting:** Full caster (CHA). Spell slots follow standard full-caster progression.

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Bardic Inspiration (d6), Spellcasting
- **Level 2:** Expertise (2 skills), Jack of All Trades (half Prof to unproficient ability checks)
- **Level 3:** Bard Subclass
- **Level 5:** Font of Inspiration -- Bardic Inspiration recharges on Short Rest; can expend a spell slot to regain one use
- **Level 7:** Countercharm -- Reaction when you or ally within 30 ft. fails a save vs. Charmed/Frightened; reroll with Advantage
- **Level 10:** Magical Secrets -- can prepare spells from Bard, Cleric, Druid, and Wizard lists
- **Level 18:** Superior Inspiration -- on Initiative, regain Bardic Inspiration uses up to 2
- **Level 20:** Words of Creation -- always have Power Word Heal and Power Word Kill prepared; can target a second creature within 10 ft.

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### College of Lore
Control and debuff specialist. Cutting Words (Level 3) uses a Reaction to expend Bardic Inspiration and subtract the die roll from an enemy's damage roll, attack roll, or ability check within 60 ft. Magical Discoveries (Level 6) grants two always-prepared spells from Cleric, Druid, or Wizard lists. Peerless Skill (Level 14) lets the Bard add a Bardic Inspiration die to their own failed ability check or attack roll (not expended on failure).
