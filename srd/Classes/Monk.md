# Monk

**Hit Die:** d8 | **Saves:** Strength, Dexterity | **Armor/Weapons:** Simple weapons, Martial Light melee weapons, no armor

## Core Mechanics

**Martial Arts** (requires no armor, no Shield, Monk weapons or Unarmed): Bonus Action Unarmed Strike, use DEX for attack/damage, damage die replaces normal weapon/unarmed damage.

| Level | Martial Arts Die | Focus Points | Unarmored Movement |
|-------|------------------|--------------|--------------------|
| 1     | 1d6              | --           | --                 |
| 2-4   | 1d6              | 2-4          | +10 ft.            |
| 5-10  | 1d8              | 5-10         | +10 to +20 ft.    |
| 11-16 | 1d10             | 11-16        | +20 to +25 ft.    |
| 17-20 | 1d12             | 17-20        | +25 to +30 ft.    |

**Unarmored Defense:** AC = 10 + DEX mod + WIS mod.

**Focus Points** (recharge all on Short/Long Rest, save DC = 8 + WIS mod + Prof):
- **Flurry of Blows** (1 pt.): 2 Unarmed Strikes as Bonus Action (3 at level 10)
- **Patient Defense** (free or 1 pt.): Bonus Action Disengage (free) or Disengage + Dodge (1 pt.; also grants Martial Arts die x2 Temp HP at level 10)
- **Step of the Wind** (free or 1 pt.): Bonus Action Dash (free) or Disengage + Dash with doubled jump (1 pt.; can carry a willing Small/Medium creature at level 10)

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Martial Arts, Unarmored Defense
- **Level 2:** Monk's Focus (Flurry/Patient Defense/Step of the Wind), Unarmored Movement, Uncanny Metabolism (on Initiative, regain all Focus Points + heal Monk level + Martial Arts die; 1/Long Rest)
- **Level 3:** Deflect Attacks (Reaction: reduce B/P/S attack damage by 1d10 + DEX mod + Monk level; if reduced to 0, spend 1 pt. to reflect 2x Martial Arts die + DEX mod damage, DEX save), Monk Subclass
- **Level 4:** Slow Fall (Reaction: reduce fall damage by 5x Monk level)
- **Level 5:** Extra Attack, Stunning Strike (1 pt., 1/turn on hit: CON save or Stunned until start of your next turn; on success, half Speed and Advantage on next attack against target)
- **Level 6:** Empowered Strikes -- Unarmed Strikes can deal Force damage
- **Level 7:** Evasion (DEX saves: no damage on success, half on fail)
- **Level 10:** Heightened Focus (improved Flurry/Patient Defense/Step of the Wind), Self-Restoration (remove Charmed/Frightened/Poisoned at end of each turn)
- **Level 13:** Deflect Energy -- Deflect Attacks works against all damage types
- **Level 14:** Disciplined Survivor -- proficiency in all saves; spend 1 Focus Point to reroll a failed save
- **Level 15:** Perfect Focus -- on Initiative (if not using Uncanny Metabolism), regain Focus Points up to 4
- **Level 18:** Superior Defense (3 pts., 1 min): Resistance to all damage except Force
- **Level 20:** Body and Mind -- DEX and WIS increase by 4 (max 25)

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Warrior of the Open Hand
Unarmed combat master. Open Hand Technique (Level 3) adds a rider to each Flurry of Blows hit: Addle (no Opportunity Attacks), Push (STR save or pushed 15 ft.), or Topple (DEX save or Prone). Wholeness of Body (Level 6, Bonus Action) heals Martial Arts die + WIS mod HP, WIS mod uses/Long Rest. Fleet Step (Level 11) allows free Step of the Wind after any other Bonus Action. Quivering Palm (Level 17, 4 pts.) sets lethal vibrations on Unarmed Strike hit; end as an action for CON save or 10d12 Force damage (half on success).
