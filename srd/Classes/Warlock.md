# Warlock

**Hit Die:** d8 | **Saves:** Wisdom, Charisma | **Armor/Weapons:** Light armor, Simple weapons

## Core Mechanics

**Pact Magic:** Short-rest caster (CHA). Few spell slots, all at the same level, recharge on Short or Long Rest.

| Level | Spell Slots | Slot Level | Invocations |
|-------|-------------|------------|-------------|
| 1     | 1           | 1st        | 1           |
| 2     | 2           | 1st        | 3           |
| 3-4   | 2           | 2nd        | 3           |
| 5-6   | 2           | 3rd        | 5           |
| 7-8   | 2           | 4th        | 6           |
| 9-10  | 2           | 5th        | 7           |
| 11-16 | 3           | 5th        | 7-9         |
| 17-20 | 4           | 5th        | 9-10        |

**Mystic Arcanum** (Level 11+): One free cast each (1/Long Rest) of a chosen spell at levels 6, 7, 8, and 9 (gained at Warlock levels 11, 13, 15, 17 respectively).

**Eldritch Invocations** (key combat options):
- **Agonizing Blast** (Level 2): Add CHA mod to a damaging cantrip's damage
- **Repelling Blast** (Level 2): Push Large or smaller 10 ft. on cantrip attack hit
- **Devil's Sight** (Level 2): See normally in magical/nonmagical darkness, 120 ft.
- **Armor of Shadows**: Cast Mage Armor on self at will
- **Pact of the Blade**: Conjure a melee weapon; use CHA for attack/damage; can deal Necrotic/Psychic/Radiant
- **Thirsting Blade** (Level 5, Pact of the Blade): Extra Attack with pact weapon
- **Eldritch Smite** (Level 5, Pact of the Blade): Expend Pact slot for +1d8 Force per slot level on hit, Prone if Huge or smaller
- **Lifedrinker** (Level 9, Pact of the Blade): +1d6 Necrotic/Psychic/Radiant 1/turn, regain HP via Hit Die
- **Devouring Blade** (Level 12, Thirsting Blade): Pact weapon gets two extra attacks (3 total)
- **Pact of the Chain**: Find Familiar at will; familiar can attack using your Attack action
- **Investment of the Chain Master** (Level 5): Familiar uses your save DC, Bonus Action attacks, can deal Necrotic/Radiant
- **Pact of the Tome**: 3 cantrips + 2 ritual spells from any list
- **Gift of the Protectors** (Level 9, Pact of the Tome): CHA mod creatures can drop to 1 HP instead of 0, 1/Long Rest
- **Witch Sight** (Level 15): Truesight 30 ft.

## Key Features by Level

- **Level 1:** Eldritch Invocations (1), Pact Magic
- **Level 2:** Magical Cunning -- 1-min rite to regain up to half max Pact slots (1/Long Rest)
- **Level 3:** Warlock Subclass
- **Level 9:** Contact Patron -- free Contact Other Plane 1/Long Rest (auto-succeed on save)
- **Level 11:** Mystic Arcanum (level 6 spell)
- **Level 13:** Mystic Arcanum (level 7 spell)
- **Level 15:** Mystic Arcanum (level 8 spell)
- **Level 17:** Mystic Arcanum (level 9 spell)
- **Level 20:** Eldritch Master -- Magical Cunning restores ALL Pact slots

## Subclasses (Level 3)

### Fiend Patron
Offensive blaster with survivability. Dark One's Blessing (Level 3) grants CHA mod + Warlock level Temp HP when you or a nearby ally reduces an enemy to 0 HP. Always-prepared spells include Burning Hands, Command, Scorching Ray, Suggestion, Fireball, Stinking Cloud, Fire Shield, Wall of Fire, Geas, Insect Plague. Dark One's Own Luck (Level 6) adds 1d10 to an ability check or save (CHA mod uses/Long Rest). Fiendish Resilience (Level 10) grants Resistance to a chosen damage type (not Force), changeable on rest. Hurl Through Hell (Level 14, 1/turn on hit): CHA save or target disappears, takes 8d10 Psychic (non-Fiends), Incapacitated until end of your next turn. 1/Long Rest or spend a Pact slot.
